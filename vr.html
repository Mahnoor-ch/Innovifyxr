What is Virtual Reality (VR)?

Virtual Reality (VR)is a fully immersive digital experience that places users in a 3D virtual environment using headsets like Oculus, HTC Vive, or Meta Quest. Unlike AR, which enhances the real world, VR completely replaces it—allowing users to interact with lifelike simulations in a safe and controlled space.

How Long Does It Take to Develop a VR Project?

The development timeline depends on the complexity and features of the project. Simple VR demos can take a few weeks, while advanced simulations or training modules may take a few months. We follow an agile process to ensure fast, flexible, and high-quality delivery.
